FAULT TOLERANCE TRONG MICROSERVICES

Giai thich thuat ngu:
Fault Tolerance trong microservice la "kha nang chiu loi" trong kin truc phan tan cua toan bo he thong ung dung.

Y nghia:
KHa nang chiu loi nghia la khi mot mat xich xay ra su co hoac loi ngoai y muon th ung dung van co the hoat dong tiep tuc
va khong bi gian doan.

Tam quan trong:
Trong mot he thong ung dung phan tan microservice, moi mot service la mot tien trinh hoat dong doc lap voi domain business
doc lap, DB doc lap, tich hop cong nghe doc lap...vv nham phuc vu cho muc dich cu the cua service do.

Demo tren la mot dien hinh cho ung dung co kien truc phan tan, moi mot service deu co muc dich va business doc lap, neu 1
trong cac service trong qua trinh hoat dong nhung phat sinh su co khong mong muon thi viec goi du lieu tu FE hoac viec
giao tiep giua cac service co the bi gian doan hoac gay ra loi hoac co the crash ung dung va day la dieu khong mong muon
trong mo hinh he thong phan tan.

Trong qua trinh van hanh hoac bao tri he thong, mot so nguyen nhan thuong gay ra loi hoac su co cho thong nhu:
    1. Network Latency - do tre mang
       Thuong la loi nguon cua phat sinh trong moi service, loi nay la nguyen nhan gay ra cac loi timeout, retry hoac la
       loi day chuyen (cascading failure)

    2. Deployment issues
       Loi do trong qua trinh trien khai do cau hinh sai, version khong tuong thich giua cac phu thuoc qua lai cua moi
       service hoac trien khai bi loi.

    3. Increased Complexity
       Khi he thong phan tan ngay mot phinh to thi do phuc tap tuong ung cung tang dan theo thoi gian

Khi trien khai mo hinh he thong phan tan, cac ky thuat lien quan den kha nang chiu loi can phai trien khai cho mot so tinh
huong co the xay ra, trong do:
    1. Fault Isolation (Co lap/ cach ly loi)
       Trong qua trinh hoat dong, khi loi phat sinh thi loi duoc cach ly va co lap sao cho loi khong lay lan va duoc giai
       quyet trong pham vi duoc cach ly an toan.

    2. Kha nang dan hoi va tu phuc hoi
       He thong co kha nang dan hoi nghia la he thong co kha nang tang va can bang theo tai. Dieu nay giup cho he thong
       co kha nang mo rong theo nhu cau thuc te tu nguoi dung.

       Khi phat trien he thong phan tan thi viec xay ra loi trong qua trinh hoat dong la dieu khong the tranh khoi, va he
       thong can phai duoc chuan bi va co ke hoach de phong ngua cac kha nang co the xay ra trong tuong lai.


Mot so ky thuat de thuc hien xu ly cho kha nang chiu loi
    1. Retries
       Day la ky thuat duoc thuc hien cho phep gui lai yeu cau trong pham vi so lan nhat dinh khi mot yeu cau duoc gui
       den mot service cu va co the phat sinh loi tam thoi nhu timeout (thoi gian cho) do ket noi mang hoac do du lieu
       nguon khong co san hoac do mot so loi tuong ung mang tinh tam thoi khong gay nghiem trong.

       Vi du: Khi FE hoac service giao tiep thuc hien gui mot yeu cau toi service A, neu service A loi thi he thong tu
              dong thuc hien thu lai yeu cau, viec nay giup cho yeu cau duoc ly va phan hoi thanh cong neu nhu loi tai
              service A mang tinh tam thoi va khong nghiem trong.

    2. Rate Limiting:
       Gioi han toc do, nghia la gioi han va kiem soat so luong request ma 1 service co the xu ly trong mot khung thoi
       gian cu the. Dieu nay giup ngan ngua kha nang gay qua tai va ap luc cho tung service, dam bao viec su dung tai
       nguyen cua service duoc can bang.

       Moi mot service trong he thong phan tan deu co dung luong nhat dinh (ram, cache, storage...), rate limiting huu ich
       trong viec dam bao cac request gui den services phu hop dung luong thuc te cua moi service va khong vuot qua muc
       cho phep.

       Rate limiting co the han che phan nao mot luong lon request co kha nang qua tai hoac cac cuoc tan cong (DDOS, pom
       request dan den viec tu choi dich vu tu cua cac request khac, day duoc xem la mot cuoc tan cong mang co chu dich
       nham lam treo he thong may chu).

    3. Bulkheads
       Vach ngan/ ngan chan, day la mot hinh thuc co lap sao cho khi mot service phat sinh loi nhung loi duoc cach ly mot
       cach an toan va khong anh huong hoac lay lan den cac service khac dang hoat dong.

    4. Circuit breaker
       Cau dao (bo ngat mach), co chuc nang bat va tat, on/off. Trong qua trinh ung dung hoat dong, he thong can phai co
       kha nang giam sat tien trinh tung service rieng va chung va ngan chan cac loi lan truyen.

       Vi du: 2 service A va B, He thong phai giam sat duoc tinh trang cua 2 service nay va phat hien loi neu nhu 1 trong
              2 service gay ra loi hoac ca 2 phat sinh loi. Neu loi phat sinh tu service B thi loi nay khong nen gay anh
              huong den service A hoac nau A va B cung loi thi loi phai can duoc duoc tach biet loi thuoc ve moi ben.

       Khi mot request gui den service can duoc xu ly, bo ngat mach dam bao sao cho request duoc xu ly mot cach tron tru.
       Co che hoat dong cua bo ngat mach nhu sau:
              Khi mot dich vu xay ra loi hoac gap su co lien tuc hoac do tre cao thi bo ngat mach se duoc kich hoat va cac
              request tiep theo se duoc chuyen den phuong an du phong da duoc khai bao hoac phan hoi loi, dieu nay giup
              cho service dang gap su co co the giam tai o muc can thiet nhung van co the cung cap dich vu nhung trai
              co the bi suy giam dong thoi dam bao ung dung hoat dong on dinh khong gian doan.

    5. Fallbacks
       Phuong an du phong, la cac hanh dong du phong khi mot service khong co kha nang cung cap dich vu hoac phan hoi.
       Phuong an du phong co the can phai dinh nghia truoc nhu cac gia tri mac dinh, hoac phan hoi mac dinh tu bo nho
       cache hoac nguon du lieu phu...vv.

       Phuong an du phong co the la phuuong an rieng cho moi service, dong thoi phuong an du phong tong the cho toan bo,
       dieu nay giup cho nguoi dung co the nhan duoc phan hoi ngay khi dich vu chinh khong co kha nang phan hoi.

    6. Timeout
       Thoi gian cho phan hoi tu he thong ve phia may khach, ngoai viec he thong tu xu ly timeout, phia FE cung can xu ly
       timeout cho ca request de dam bao request duoc xu ly tron tru.

    7. Graceful Degradation
       Giam hieu suat mot cach nhe nhang, ky thuat nay de cap den van de he thong hoat dong binh thuong nhung cac chuc
       nang gap su co hoac qua tai. Cac tinh nang khong thiet yeu hoac ton nhieu tai nguyen co the duoc vo hieu hoa hoac
       don gian hoa de nhuong cho cho cac tinh nang quan trong hon => day la y nghia cua viec giam hieu suat mot cach kheo
       leo va giup ngan ngua loi dam bao he thong hoat dong manh me va co kha nang chiu loi.

Trong he sinh thai Spring, thu viec Resilience4J la mot thu vien cung cap cac ky thuat cho kha nang chiu loi cua he thong
day la thu vien nhe va se su dung.


RateLimiting va cac ly do can phai su dung RateLimiting
Nhu da giai thich trong phan kha nang chiu loi va RateLimiting la mot phan trong van de xu ly kha nang chiu loi cua mot
he thong phan tan giup service co the gioi han toc do, kiem soat so luong request va xu ly so luong request trong mot khung
thoi gian nhat dinh. Vay, mot so cac ly do can phai dung den ky thuat RateLimiting nhu sau:

    1. Preventing Abuse - ngan chan hanh vi lam dung
       Mot so truong hop tan cong qua duong truyen mang va gay ra van de tu choi dich vu, cac cuoc tan cong nay duoc xem
       la dang tan cong DDDOS (Distributed Denial Of Service).

       Vi du:
               Gia su rang ta co may chu server A, server A co the nhan bat ky request nao tu phia client

               Nguoi dung thong thuong co the gui request yeu cau trich xuat du lieu tu server A mot cach binh thuong.

               Nhung, voi DDOS no la mot tap hop cac thiet bi, trong do gom mot may chu dieu khien va nhieu thiet bi ve tinh.
               Cac thiet bi ve tinh la cac thiet bi trong to chuc cua toi pham DDOS chu dong su dung phan mem remote tu phia
               may chu DDOS hoac cung co the la cac may nguoi dung binh thuong tro thanh nan nhan khi vo tinh cai dat chuong
               trinh remote cua may chu DDOS.

               ------------
               | Attacker |  May chu DDOS (control)
               ------------

                              ----------
                              | Zombie | remote --------->
                              ----------

                              ----------                           ----------      -----------             ------------
                              | Zombie | remote --------->         | Router |   -> | Router  |     -->     | Server A |
                              ----------                           ----------      -----------             ------------

                              ----------
                              | Zombie | remote --------->
                              ----------


               Khi DDOS nham muc tieu la Server A va tao ra nhieu request tu cac thiet bi ve tinh cua DDOS (Zombie Machine)
               sao cho so luong request vuot dung luong ma bang thong do co the chiu dung nham gay ra viec tu choi dich vu
               tu Server A truoc khi cac request cua nguoi dung binh thuong co the den duoc Server A, neu so luong request
               tro nen bung phat dan den Server A co the system down ngay lap tuc.

               =>> RateLimiting phan nao co the xu ly duoc van de so luong request va giam toc request khi can thiet.

    2. Resource Allocation - Phan bo nguon tai nguyen
       Viec phan bo nguon tai nguyen giup cho he thong co the can bang viec phan bo nguyen tai nguyen mot cach hop ly den
       nguoi dung.

    3. Cost Management - Quan ly chi phi
       Quan ly va co the toi uu hoa chi phi neu he thong dat tren cloud va he thong phai tra chi phi cho cac request tu
       clients.

    4. Web Scraping
       Ngan chan hanh vi sao chep du lieu tu he thong

    5. Login Attemp
       Ngan chan cac hanh vi co gang thu nghiem xac thuc danh tinh nguoi dung tu dong bang cac proxy server nhu burp suite...

De co the su dung combo Resilience4J phuc vu cho khang chiu loi cua he thong, can phai cau hinh bang cach them cac goi
phu thuoc va khai bao cau hinh cho application.yml hoac application.properties.

de co the thuc nghiem mo phong cho tinh nang giam toc va quan ly so luong request RateLimiting, can su dung cong cu ho
tro JMeter. JMeter la cong cu gia lap tao ra nhieu request tan cong vao he thong de kich hoat chuc nang RateLimiting. De
su dung JMeter can phai tai Apache JMeter hoac co the dung docker de thu nghiem JMeter
