Service "Service_mvc" la service duoc thiet ke theo kieu Monolithic, day la service dunng de test luong du lieu khi su
dung dich Authentication Service xac thuc danh tinh nguoi dung

Luong thuc thi Service_mvc
Gia su rang Service_mvc su dung dich vu xac thuc danh tinh nguoi dung tu Authentication Service va duoc cap authServiceId sau khi
dang ky su dung dich nhu da huong dan trong file huong-dan-su-dung.txt trong service authentication service.

Khi nguoi dung thuc hien xac thuc local va tuong tac tren template engine Thymleaf Spring, BE nhan du lieu khi nguoi dung submit va
toan bo du lieu nay duoc BE gui truc tiep den service Authentication Service dong thoi BE phai gui kem theo authServiceId trong request
bang RestTemplate.

Khi nguoi dung click vao nut bam "Login with Google", thay vi "service_mvc" dieu huong nguoi dung ve UI dang nhap mac dinh cua google
thi service_mvc dieu huong ve URL do Authentication service cung cap, URL nay se tu dong render UI cua Authentication Service thiet ke.

Dung tai UI dang nhap bang google cho Authentication Servie thiet ke, luc nay nguoi dung dang nhap tai khoan dang nhap email cua nguoi dung
va Authentication Service lay email cung voi gooogle Client ID ma Authentication Service da dang ky dich vu OAuth
