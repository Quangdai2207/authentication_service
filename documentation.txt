Spring microservice mini

Spring microservice mini la du an duoc trien khai theo kien truc Microservices, gom cac services:
    1. Service authentication_service       -> Xac thuc va phan quyen cho cac ung dung monolithic & microservice
    2. Service_mvc                          -> Service test thu nghiem MVC dang ky xac thuc voi authentication service
    3. reaction                             -> Dinh nghia cac doi tuong cho cac service giap tiep qua OpenFeing hoac Restemplate hoac
                                               message queue nhu Kafka, RabbitMQ
    4. service_registry                     -> Dich vu quan ly service strong he thong ung dung microservices
                                               Service Registry su dung Eureka Service de quan ly microservice
    5. manage_config                        -> Dong vai tro la may chu quan ly cau hinh cho toan bo ung dung microservices
                                               Su dung Github de quan ly cac cau hinh cua microservice.
                                               manage_config mac du la mot service quan ly cau hinh chung cua toan bo he thong microservice
                                               nhung no van duoc xem la mot microservice.


    Cac services deu duoc tich hop Spring Actuator de quan ly so lieu he thong va tinh trang suc khoe cua moi he thong
    Cac services deu duoc su dung Zipkin de quan ly va truy vet du lieu theo Trace va Span trong qua trinh truyen va nhan du lieu. Zipkin
    la mot may chu hoat dong doc lap va cac services phai tu dang ky de su dung dich vu truy vet du lieu tu Zipkin.
