manage_config service

manage_service dong vai tro la mot may chu quan ly cac cau hinh cua toan bo services trong toan he thong ung dung
microservice.
manage_service mac du la may chu nhung van duoc xem la mot service trong kien truc microservice.
mange_service su dung dependency "config server" cuac Spring Boot de thuc hien quan ly cau hinh.

CO CHE HOAT DONG CUA SPRING CONFIG SERVER
    Spring Config Server quan ly cau hinh thong qua cloud nhu git hub, aws...vv, toan bo cau hinh cua toan bo
    microservice trong he thong deu duoc quan ly thong qua cloud.

    Thong thuong, moi service trong kien truc microservice deu co file cau hinh rieng la application.properties
    hoac application.yml de thuc hien cau hinh cac bien moi truong hoac cau hinh tich hop cong nghe...vv. Nhung
    khi dang ky quan ly cau hinh tu Spring Config Server thi toan bo noi dung cau hinh trong cac file cau hinh
    rieng le cua tung services se duoc khai bao trong noi dung file cua repo cloud. Thay do cac service chi can
    khi bao mot so thong tin chinh trong file cau hinh application.properties hoac application.yml de khi service
    khoi dong thi Spring lay cac thong tin do khai bao voi repo cloud de lay cau hinh cho service do.

        Vi du: trong project spring_microservice_mini la mot du an duoc build theo kien truc microservice, va service manage_config
               la service dong vai tro la may chu quan ly toan bo cau hinh cho cac service khac. Va manage_config hoat dong dua tren
               framework Config Server cua Spring.

               Tai service manage_config dong vai tro la may chu va muon thuc hien quan ly cau hinh bang cloud, can phai them cac phu thuoc
               va cau hinh nhu sau:
               Them phu thuoc cho vai tro la may chu voi dependency
                   <dependency>
                       <groupId>org.springframework.boot</groupId>
                       <artifactId>spring-boot-starter-actuator</artifactId>
                   </dependency>
                   <dependency>
                       <groupId>org.springframework.cloud</groupId>
                       <artifactId>spring-cloud-config-server</artifactId>
                   </dependency>

                Trong application.properties cua manage_config them noi dung cau hinh:
                    spring.application.name=manage-config
                    server.port=9999
                    eureka.client.service-url-defaultZone=http://localhost:8761/eureka
                    eureka.client.register-with-eureka=true
                    eureka.client.fetch-registry=true
                    management.tracingsamplingprobability=1.0

                    ## URL lien ket voi Git Cloud quan ly cau hinh
                    spring.cloud.config.server.git.uri=https://github.com/Quangdai2207/Spring-config


               Phia client service service_registry muon Config Server quan ly cau hinh thi phai thuc hien dang ky thong qua service manage_config.
               De co the dang ky quan ly cau hinh service_registry can phai them dependency Config Client phia may khach
                   <dependency>
                       <groupId>org.springframework.cloud</groupId>
                       <artifactId>spring-cloud-starter-config</artifactId>
                   </dependency>

               sau khi them phu thuoc, trong application.properties cua service_registry can phai them cac thuoc tinh cau hinh bat buoc truoc khi
               doc cau hinh tu cloud repo nhu sau:
                    spring.application.name=service-registry
                    server.port=8761
                    spring.profile.active=dev

                    ## Quan ly cau hinh cloud tu manage_config
                    spring.config.import=configserver:http://localhost:9999

                    Nhu vay, Neu nhu service_registry muon su dung hoan toan cau hinh tu cloud do manage_config quan ly thi toan bo noi
                    dung trong application.properties chi can khai bao ten service, port va profile va dong thoi service_registry cung khong
                    can phai tao cac profile application-dev.properties, application-prod.properties hay application-qa.properties, cac noi dung
                    nay Spring Cconfig Server se tu dong lay trong repo cloud de ap dung.

                    Tru truong hop neu service_registry doi khi muon dung cau hinh tu cloud doi khi muon dung cau hinh local cua service thi
                    service co the them noi dung cau hinh truc tiep cho application.properties dong thoi co the tao them cac profiles de spring
                    thu thi tren cau hinh local cua service va trong application.properties tai phan noi dung cau hinh config can phai them tu khoa
                    "optional" de thuc hien dieu do.

                     # Them "optional" de co the ap dung noi dung cau hinh local cua service
                     spring.config.import=optional:configserver:http://localhost:9999

               buoc tiep theo, sau khi khai bao cac noi dung chinh cho application.properties nhu ten service, port, profile. Tren cloud repo (Github)
               can phai thuc hien cac noi dung sau:

                    +   Tao repo cau hinh (Vi du: config-repo)
                    +   Tao cac file profile cau hinh cho cac service, trong vi du service_registry dang ky su dung cau hinh tu cloud thi can phai
                        tao profile cau hinh va noi dung cau hinh cho tung profile.

                        Ten cua file cau hinh tren repo cloud tot nhat nen cung voi ten service name "service-registry" va cac profile chi can
                        service-registry-dev, service-registry-prod...vv, gieu nay giup cho viec quan ly cac file cau hinh hoat dong dung neu nhu
                        co nhieu service dang ky quan ly cau hinh chung

               (Xem demo tai service_registry va cac file cau hinh cho service_registry tai repo githhub https://github.com/Quangdai2207/Spring-config)


               Mot luu y quan trong trong vi du nay la manage_config va service_registry deu la 2 service dong vai tro la may chu. Trong do, voi
               manage_config la service may chu quan ly cau hinh chung cua toan bo he thong microservice va service_registry la service may chu quan
               ly toan bo cac services trong microservice, khi ca 2 may chu phu thuoc lan nhau voi mot ben la client va mot ben la server thi khi khoi
               dong de xay ra tinh trang bao loi khi khong ket noi duoc dich vu cua service dong vai tro la may chu.

                    Giai thich:
                        Voi quan ly cau hinh thi manage_config dong vai tro la may chu va service_registry dong vai tro la may khach
                        Voi quan ly service thi service_registry dong vai tro la may chu va manage_config la may khach

                        Nhu vay khii khoi dong 1 trong 2 service thi rat de xay ra bao loi client khong ket noi duoc voi may chu.

                    Cach giai quyet:
                        Neu service-registry dong vai tro la may chu quan ly toan bo services cua he thong va manage_config dang ky dich vu voi tu cach
                        la Client cho Cloud Eureka tu Service-registry, thi toan bo noi dung cau hinh chinh cua Eureka vva cac noi dung co lien quan khac
                        nen duoc dat tai file cau hinh local cua Service-registry, nhung noi dung mang tinh khong rang buoc 2 ben co the up len repo cloud.
                        Chieu nguoc lai service-registry dang ky quan ly cau hinh tu mange_config cung xu ly tuong tu voi manage_config.


                        Vay khi thuc hien dang ky cau hinh, nen kiem tra van de nhu da giai thich de quyet dinh co dang ky cau hinh hay khong. Neu ca 2
                        khong phu thuoc qua lai thi co the thuc hien dang ky cau hinh, nhung neu co dau hieu phu thuoc qua lai thi nen can nhac viec them
                        tu khoa "optional" cho service phu thuoc dang ky quan ly cau hinh.

               Mot luu y nua la tai cac file cau hinh cua cac service khi dang ky quan ly cau hinh chung voi vai tro la may khach
               spring.config.import=configserver:http://localhost:9999 Spring Config Server se tu dong lay toan bo noi dung cau hinh tren cloud de
               ap dung cho service tuong ung. Tuy nhien, Spring Config Server co the cho phep them thuoc tinh "optional" nhu sau
               spring.config.import=optional:configserver:http://localhost:9999. Khi khai bao thuoc tinh "optional" dieu nay co nghia khi Service
               khoi dong lan dau, Spring se lay cau hinh tren cloud hoac trong qua trinh service dang hoat dong ma noi dung cau hinh tren cloud co
               the loi hoac chet thi Spring tu dong lay noi dung trong file cau hinh local cua service ap dung va ung dung khong bi crash nua chung

               Nhu vay, khi thuc hien dang ky quan ly cau hinh chung, ban than tung service nen co noi dung cau hinh tuong ung voi cau hinh dang
               ky quan ly de dam bao cac su co xay ra khong mong muon.
